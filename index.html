    MSME Manthan 2024 is not just an event—it’s a nationwide movement to empower, uplift, and enable India’s Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) to reach their full potential. Organized by the Chamber of Indian MSME, this premier platform brings together visionary entrepreneurs, financial institutions, government bodies, and industry leaders to address real-world challenges and develop sustainable, growth-driven solutions.

    Now in its latest edition, MSME Manthan has evolved into a dynamic hub of innovation, networking, and thought leadership. The event focuses on key drivers of MSME transformation—access to finance, cash-flow based lending, collateral-free loans, delayed payment recovery, digital adoption, and policy-level interventions. It also presents valuable insights for startups, helping them navigate the complexities of business growth and funding.

    Objective

    The MSME Manthan Awards aim to celebrate, recognize, and empower outstanding Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) and visionary entrepreneurs who are driving innovation, creating jobs, and contributing to India’s economic growth.

    The awards go beyond recognition—they are designed to inspire and spotlight the resilience, ingenuity, and impact of MSMEs that have overcome challenges, adopted transformational practices, and set new benchmarks in business excellence.

    By honoring trailblazers across diverse sectors, the MSME Manthan Awards strive to:

    Encourage best practices in business innovation, financial discipline, and sustainability

    Promote access to finance, digital adoption, and inclusive growth

    Highlight inspiring stories of MSMEs making a difference in local and global markets

    Strengthen the MSME ecosystem by fostering collaboration between entrepreneurs, investors, and policymakers

    Position award winners as role models for emerging and aspiring MSME entrepreneurs

    The objective is not just to reward success, but to create a ripple effect of motivation and knowledge-sharing that fuels the rise of India’s next generation of MSME leaders.

    AWARDS

    Entrepreneurs are nominated by subcommittee members, after which they undergo a comprehensive evaluation based on predetermined standards.

    The subcommittee will proceed to select entrepreneurs who meet the criteria for inclusion on a shortlist for further consideration. Following this, the team will submit its shortlisted recommendations to the grand jury, along with detailed reports and justifications for each nominee.

    Grand jury members conduct independent reviews and evaluations of each nominee and select winners under each category.

    The award ceremony announces the winners through an award ceremony.
